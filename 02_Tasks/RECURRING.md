# RECURRING

## Purpose
Tracks repeating tasks that happen on a cadence (daily, weekly, monthly). Ensures consistency and prevents important habits from slipping.

## How to Use
- Check this first thing in the morning (daily items)
- On Friday, scan weekly items and schedule time
- Update "Last Done" immediately after completing task
- Update "Next Due" to calculate the next occurrence
- Move completed daily items to DONE_LOG at EOD
- Any task overdue by > 2 cycles should get review with manager

## Daily Routine Templates

### Morning (9:00am - 15min)
1. Check TODAY.md top 3 priorities
2. Review WAITING_ON for follow-ups due today
3. Scan calendar for calls/meetings
4. Identify biggest blocker and plan mitigation
5. Start first "must do" item

### Evening (5:30pm - 10min)
1. Log completed tasks to DONE_LOG
2. Move incomplete items to BACKLOG or reschedule
3. Quick wins reflection (what went well?)
4. Prep top 3 for tomorrow (add to TODAY.md)
5. Close email, clear desk

---

## Tracking Notes
- **Overdue items**: Any task not done by 2 days past "Next Due" gets flagged
- **Seasonal**: Add monthly reviews once stable (weekly reviews suffice for ramp)
- **Adjustment**: After 2 weeks, reassess frequency of weekly tasks
- **Blockers**: If recurring task blocked, note in WAITING_ON instead of skipping

---

## Escalation
- Miss daily routine 2x: Review with manager (workload issue?)
- Miss weekly review 2x: Manager escalation (priority/workload adjustment)
- Memory files stale > 2 weeks: Loss of context, escalate
