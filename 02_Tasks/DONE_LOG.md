# DONE_LOG

## Purpose
Historical record of completed work. Tracks what was accomplished, when, and what resulted. Useful for 1:1s, retrospectives, and demonstrating progress during ramp period.

## How to Use
- Add entries daily during shutdown routine (5:30pm)
- Include date, task name, output/result, and brief notes
- Move items here from TODAY.md once checked off
- Review this weekly (Friday retrospective) to assess productivity
- Use monthly summaries to prepare for manager reviews
- Celebrate wins—this is your progress tracker

## Tips for Effective Logging
- **Be specific**: "Contacted 5 prospects" > "Outreach"
- **Include outputs**: Links to documents, CRM entries, etc.
- **Note learnings**: What worked, what didn't (useful for retrospectives)
- **Track time**: Rough hours spent helps calibrate effort over time
- **Celebrate**: Don't skip the wins—they matter for morale
