# Opportunity Tracking Template

**Purpose:** Track individual deal progress, stakeholders, and risk factors from early engagement through close.

**How to Use:**
- Create one opportunity file per active deal or major customer initiative
- Update stage and activity log after every meaningful interaction
- Review risk factors and win themes monthly
- Use next steps section to maintain momentum and clear ownership
- Archive when won, lost, or stale >90 days
