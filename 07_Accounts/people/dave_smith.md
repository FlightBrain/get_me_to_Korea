### The Dexcom Formula (Dave's Email Style)
**Structure:**
1. **Opening Hook:** Press/financial signal relevant to their business
2. **Connection to Pain:** How that signal relates to what they likely care about (AI, automation, scale)
3. **2-3 Specific Bullets:** Data-driven observations (from financial reports, earnings, announcements)
4. **No CTA:** Ends without asking for anything—invites response by being smart

**Example Template:**
"Hi [Name], I saw [Company] announced [Press Release]. Caught my eye because [Financial Insight]. Means you're probably thinking about [Challenge]. Most companies in your situation either [Path A] or [Path B]. Few get [Outcome]. Worth a quick call to see what's on your roadmap?"

**Key Principle:** The email IS the value—don't ask for the meeting, just put smart insight on the table.
