## Notes for SDRs

- **Voice AI angle:** Navan (0.9+ F1 score, hundreds of daily calls). Do not use Navan for non-voice use cases.
- **Search/RAG/conversational AI:** Dropbox (10K+ tests, under 10 min eval per PR).
- **Scaling across large eng org:** Notion (under 24hr model deploy, 70 engineers).
- **AI accuracy improvement:** Zapier (50% to 90%+ accuracy in 2-3 months).
- **Observability/copilot:** Retool (25% accuracy improvement).
- **EdTech:** Coursera (45x more feedback, 90% satisfaction).
- **High volume:** Replit (millions of sessions).
- **Token cost:** Fintool (1.5B tokens/day visibility).
