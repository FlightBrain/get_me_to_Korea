## Research-to-Content Process

**When to create a content idea from research:**
1. Signal appears in >2 sources (Reddit + customer call + LinkedIn = strong signal)
2. Objection appears in 3+ prospect conversations
3. Customer quotes or pain points are uniquely articulate/interesting
4. Topic aligns with Braintrust's positioning and moat

**How to develop:**
1. Capture idea in this table with source
2. Research 2-3 additional data points to support angle
3. Draft outline with target audience and unique angle
4. Share with marketing for prioritization
5. Update status as it moves through workflow
