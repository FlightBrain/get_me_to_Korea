## Signal Categories & Patterns

### High-Intent Signals (Act within 1 week)
- Hiring for eval, safety, or quality assurance roles
- Public mention of evaluation or hallucination problems
- Company launches AI product/feature publicly
- Executive hire focused on AI governance

### Medium-Intent Signals (Research & track)
- Company fundraising round (Series A/B)
- Tech stack adoption of LLM frameworks
- Industry trend articles mentioning AI reliability
- Competitive feature launches

### Validation Signals (Build narrative)
- Public statements about AI quality concerns
- Panel discussions on AI safety/reliability
- Research papers citing evaluation challenges
- News coverage of AI failures/incidents
