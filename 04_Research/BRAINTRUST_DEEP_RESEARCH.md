## 1. PRODUCT: What Braintrust Is

### Category
AI observability platform.

### One-liners (approved)
- "Braintrust is the observability layer for production AI."
- "Braintrust is the AI observability platform for shipping quality AI products."
- "Braintrust is the AI observability platform helping teams measure, evaluate, and improve AI in production."

### Short Boilerplate
Braintrust is the AI observability platform for production AI. By connecting evals and observability in one workflow, teams at Notion, Stripe, Zapier, Vercel, and Ramp ship quality AI products at scale.

### What Braintrust Does (Functional Breakdown)
- **Data:** Turning traces and outputs into structured evaluation datasets
- **Observability (o11y):** Understanding model behavior in production
- **Evals:** Defining what "good" means and measuring against it
- **Iteration:** Comparing prompts, models, and versions to improve quality
- **Quality gates:** Automated checks that prevent regressions from reaching production
- **Workflow acceleration:** AI-powered tools that speed up the entire development cycle

### What Braintrust is NOT
- NOT an inference layer (does not serve models)
- NOT a framework (integrates with whatever you use)
- NOT an agent builder (helps you understand and improve agents you already built)
- NOT a guardrails product (measures outcomes, does not constrain behavior)

### Brand Narrative
Observability has been how teams understand software. Logs, metrics, and traces showed what broke and why. But AI doesn't fail with stack traces: it drifts, hallucinates, and regresses silently. That's why AI development needs a new kind of observability. AI observability adds evals and becomes the quality layer for AI. Engineers and Product Managers move beyond monitoring to actively debugging, evaluating, and improving AI together directly in production.

### Key Product Features
- **Playgrounds:** No-code experimentation for non-technical users
- **Loop:** AI assistant that writes evaluation code, semantic log queries
- **Human Review UI:** Keyboard shortcuts, annotation queues
- **Brainstore:** Sub-1s trace query database (24x faster than competitors)
- **CI/CD quality gates:** Automated regression prevention
- **Hybrid deployment:** Data never leaves VPC (enterprise)
- **SOC2, RBAC, SSO, audit logs:** Enterprise security built-in

### Voice and Tone
- Pillars: Technical, Opinionated, Proof-driven, Human
- Lead with technical credibility and features

- DO NOT say: "framework," "reliable," "simple," "MLOps"

## 6. SALES PROCESS: How Deals Move

### Sales Stages (MEDDPICC Framework)

| Stage | Goal | Owner | Exit Criteria | Key Question |
|-------|------|-------|--------------|--------------|
| **S0: Prospecting** | Create context, secure next step | SDR | AE pursues meeting beyond initial SDR call. Contact + Notes with Pain OR Interest attached | Is this account worth exploring? |
| **S1: Qualify** | Decide if real opportunity | SDR + AE | Next meeting confirmed + champion named + problem hypothesis documented | Is this worth AE + SE time? |
| **S2: Scope** | Define what success looks like | AE | Success criteria defined, pain confirmed, eval plan agreed, champion actively engaged | What problem are we solving and how will we win? |
| **S3: Prove** | Build defensible business + technical case | AE + SE | Technical win + value quantified (BVA/metrics) + EB engaged + decision process validated | Can we prove ROI and secure buyer confidence? |
| **S4: Contracting** | Get to signature | AE | Commercial terms agreed, procurement/legal processing paper, signer path confirmed | Can we execute the decision cleanly? |
| **S5: Booking Review** | Ensure revenue accuracy | AE + RevOps | All approvals complete, contract/quote uploaded, billing validated | Is this deal accurate and bookable? |
| **S6: Closed Won** | Transition to delivery | AE + CSA | Contract executed, kickoff scheduled, ownership transferred to CS | How do we deliver value fast? |

### Common Failure Modes
- S0: No context passed to AE
- S1: Meeting happens but no real intent
- S2: Scope creep or vague success criteria
- S3: Weak ROI or missing economic buyer
- S4: Legal stalls or re-trading
- S5: Data issues delay close

### AE/SDR Engagement Model (4 Non-Negotiable Components)
1. **Territory Planning:** Align on account prioritization and contact selection. Use 5x5 (5 accounts, 5 contacts each) or 10x5 framework
2. **Weekly 1x1s:** 30-min recurring sync. SDR owns the agenda. Structure: Meeting Forecast (5min), Opportunity Forecast (5min), Territory Planning (15min), Next Steps (5min)
3. **Consistent Communication:** Share all meaningful responses (good or bad) via Slack in real-time. Don't wait for weekly 1x1
4. **Intro Meeting Handoff:** Debrief within 1 day. Two things SDR needs: Growth feedback + S1 confirmation

### PG Tactics
- **Prospect Referrals:** When prospect refers to specific contact/team
- **Bottoms Up:** Start from lower contacts, uncover front-line issues, move up
- **Top Down:** Start from C-level/VP, attach to strategic goals/initiatives (use 10-K/10-Q)
- **Bi-directional:** Attack from both top-down and bottoms-up simultaneously
- **Synchronized PG:** AE and SDR attack same contacts at same account

### SDR Compensation
- SDRs compensated on intro meetings (S0s) that convert to S1
- The intro meeting is the most important meeting for an SDR

## 8. OBJECTION HANDLING

### PII Data / EU / GDPR Objection
**Scenario:** Customer (especially DACH/EU-based) doesn't want to send production logs/traces containing PII into Braintrust.

**Key Messaging:** Instrumenting AI into Braintrust improves system quality, reliability, and safety. NOT employee monitoring.

**Privacy-Preserving Approach:**
1. Pseudonymize at the edge (client side): Replace employee identifiers with random session IDs or salted hashes
2. Data minimization + masking: Client-side redaction before sending. SDK supports masking rules
3. Access restrictions (RBAC): Limit to authorized technical roles
4. Short retention: Configurable retention windows
5. Purpose limitation: "No HR / no productivity / no discipline" use

**Data Residency:**
- Hybrid/self-hosted data plane: Logs/datasets stay in customer's EU infrastructure
- Braintrust control plane orchestrates
