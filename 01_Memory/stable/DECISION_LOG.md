## Decision-Making Framework

Use this when facing a material decision:

1. **Clarify the decision:** What exactly are we deciding? (Not "how to sell better," but "should we focus on vertical X or horizontal outreach?")
2. **Gather context:** What prompted this? What does success look like?
3. **Identify constraints:** Time, budget, resources, information available
4. **List options:** At least 3 paths
5. **Evaluate trade-offs:** Speed vs. quality, learning vs. execution, short-term vs. long-term
6. **Decide:** Pick and commit (don't waffle)
7. **Set review date:** When will you evaluate if this was right? (Add to ACTIVE_CONTEXT.md)
