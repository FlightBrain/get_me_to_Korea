## Contact Status Definitions

**Stage:**
- **Prospect (Cold):** First touch, not yet qualified
- **Prospect (Warm):** Replied to outreach, initial interest
- **Prospect (Research):** Identified but not yet contacted
- **Prospect (Intro):** Introduction pending or scheduled
- **Prospect (Stuck):** In conversation but stalled; needs game plan
- **Active (Q&A/Demo/Meeting):** Scheduled or in active conversation
- **Customer:** Closed deal

**Relationship Status:**
- **New:** First touch in last 2 weeks
- **Warm:** Replied or scheduled initial conversation
- **Hot:** Active deal cycle, proposal or close stage
- **Cold:** 30+ days no activity
- **Ally:** Internal stakeholder helping facilitate introductions
- **Dead:** Unresponsive 60+ days or explicitly declined
