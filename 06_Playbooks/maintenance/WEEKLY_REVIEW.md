# WEEKLY_REVIEW.md

**Purpose:** Step back from daily execution, audit performance, clean backlogs, and plan next week strategically. 45 minutes every Friday.

**When to Use:** Friday afternoon (2-3 PM), after clearing that day's inbox.

**Inputs Needed:**
- DONE_LOG.md (this week's work)
- ACTIVE_CONTEXT.md (current deals and campaigns)
- Inbox and task backlogs
- CUSTOMER_LANGUAGE.md and SIGNALS.md (new research insights)
- Playbooks (assess if they're working)

---

## Steps (45 minutes)

1. **Review DONE_LOG This Week** (5 min)
   - Count outreaches sent, meetings booked, deals moved
   - Calculate: outreach → meeting rate (X% of outreach = meetings)
   - Calculate: meetings → next steps (Y% of calls = follow-up interest)
   - Are you hitting your targets? Above? Below?

2. **Clean & Archive Task Backlogs** (5 min)
   - Review WAITING_ON.md—anything that can be closed?
   - Review inbox of new tasks—anything to file or delete?
   - Move dead-end items to archive (didn't respond, unqualified, lost)
   - Consolidate repetitive tasks (if you've done it 3 times, make a template)

3. **Audit Inbox for Stale Items** (5 min)
   - What's been sitting in inbox >3 days without action?
   - Either: prioritize it, delegate it, or delete it
   - If you won't do it next week, don't carry it

4. **Review Prospect Pipeline & Signals** (5 min)
   - ACTIVE_CONTEXT.md—which campaigns are hot?
   - SIGNALS.md—which signals are still valid?
   - CUSTOMER_LANGUAGE.md—which pain phrases worked best?
   - Plan: double down on what's working, kill what's not

5. **Assess Outreach Performance** (5 min)
   - Which emails/channels got best response?
   - Which openers worked? Which failed?
   - Which subreddits, X accounts, LinkedIn angles generated leads?
   - Document in PLAYBOOKS (update EMAIL_WRITING, channel playbooks)

6. **Refine Playbooks** (5 min)
   - Did you discover better outreach frameworks this week?
   - New pain language to add to CUSTOMER_LANGUAGE?
   - Process improvement? Update relevant playbooks
   - Example: "Found that [pain phrase] generates 3x response. Update EMAIL_WRITING.md opener rules."

7. **Identify Repeated Tasks Worth Templating** (3 min)
   - What did you do 3+ times this week identically?
   - If yes, make a template. Example: "Comparative pricing email template"
   - File it in writing/ or channels/ for reuse

8. **Update ACTIVE_CONTEXT.md for Next Week** (3 min)
   - Confirm current campaigns, kill dead ones
   - Rank prospects by stage and temperature
   - Flag upcoming milestones ("Sarah Chen decision by Wednesday")
   - Add any new high-priority targets from research

9. **Plan Next Week** (4 min)
   - Set weekly goal: outreach volume, meetings to book, deals to close
   - Identify 1-2 campaigns to double down on
   - Identify 1 area to improve (faster research? better openers?)
   - Block time for deep work (research, writing, templates)

## Examples

**Weekly metrics review:**
- Outreach sent: 75 emails
- Response rate: 12% (9 engaged)
- Meetings booked: 3
- Conversion: 4% (75 outreaches → 3 meetings)
- Below target? Hypothesis: openers are generic. Action: test new opener next week.

**Refined playbooks:**
- Found: "Hallucination" language in 8 replies this week vs. "latency" in 2
- Update: EMAIL_WRITING.md to feature "hallucination testing" as primary hook
- Update: REDDIT_MINING.md to target r/MachineLearning threads on hallucination
- Expectation: 8→12% response rate next week

**New template created:**
- Did "comparative pricing" email 4 times this week
- Template: "PRICING_COMPARISON_EMAIL_TEMPLATE.md"
- Goal: reduce time per email from 8 min to 2 min next week

**Stale backlog cleaned:**
- Marcus (Stripe) on PTO until April 7 → moved to WAITING_ON with reminder date
- Unqualified leads from random inbound → archived, not pursuing
- 3 tasks in inbox "nice to have" → deleted, not mission-critical

**Next week plan:**
- Campaign: "ML ops teams + eval latency angle" (high signal)
- Outreach target: 80 emails, 15 LinkedIn reaches
- Goals: 4 meetings booked, 1 deal to next stage
- Improvement focus: Test 3 new subject line angles
- Deep work block: 2 hours Thursday morning for Reddit mining + subreddit research

## Common Mistakes

- **No metrics review** — You don't know if you're getting better or worse. Flying blind.
- **Not killing dead campaigns** — You carry 6 active campaigns but none are producing. Diffuse effort.
- **Skipping playbook updates** — You found "hallucination" works but don't document it. Next week you're not using it.
- **Not templating** — You write the same email 10 times. Wastes 30 min/week.
- **Overambitious next week plan** — 150 outreaches + 8 calls + 3 close attempts = you hit 1, feel defeated.
- **Planning but not linking to Monday** — You plan next week but don't tell your ACTIVE_CONTEXT for Monday startup. Plan gets ignored.

## Maintenance Notes

- **Timing:** Friday 2-3 PM is ideal—you're done with calls, inbox is cleared, brain is fresh.
- **Monthly cadence:** On 1st Friday of month, also audit how playbooks are performing overall. Any major gaps? Add new ones.
- **Seasonality:** Q2/Q3 often heavier outreach. Q4 heavier follow-up. Plan accordingly.
- **Rollover:** If a plan didn't work, don't repeat it blindly. Add to COMMON_MISTAKES section of playbook.
- **Sharing insights:** Share top 1-2 learnings with team (sales channel). Builds collective intelligence.
- **Review this playbook:** After 4 weeks, is 45 min realistic? Adjust timing and depth based on your pace.
