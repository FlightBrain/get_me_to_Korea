## 4 Use Case Patterns

### Pattern 1: Cold LinkedIn DM (Bryan connected, prospect not responding to SDR)

**Format:** 5-7 sentences, no bullets, conversational
**When:** Bryan is connected to prospect on LinkedIn, SDR has tried, no response

**Structure:**
1. Warm bridge OR specific signal about their company/role (line 1 earns attention)
2. Bryan's intro: "I lead worldwide sales at Braintrust" or "I'm Bryan and lead the GTM team at Braintrust"
3. Logos matched to their industry: "We work with [X, Y, Z] to..."
4. Specific pain tied to their role (CTO = platform reliability/governance, VP AI = accuracy/model drift, VP Eng = deployment speed)
5. Low-friction CTA: "Would love 20 minutes to swap notes on how you're doing this today"

**Approved example (Christina / Slingshot — Bryan sent without changes):**
```
Hi Adrian,

Tim mentioned we should connect with you directly. I lead worldwide sales at Braintrust. We help AI teams at Microsoft, Netflix, Instacart, Airbnb, Stripe, OpenAI, Perplexity, Replit, and many more scale LLM evals and production observability.

Given that Slingshot launched TALOS and Agatha wanted to share how we can help improve agent quality and reliability.

Would love 20 minutes to swap notes on how you are doing this today.

Best,
Bryan
```

**Why it worked:** Warm bridge (Tim), strong logo list, specific trigger (TALOS launch), clear pain angle (agent quality), direct meeting CTA.

### Pattern 2: Event Hook (Conference, Speaking Slot, HumanX)

**Format:** Can be longer, bullet topics OK here (3 max), ends with "open to meeting?"
**When:** There's an event coming up, prospect is attending or speaking

**Structure:**
1. Context: "I'm Bryan and lead the GTM team at Braintrust and have been following [Company]'s [specific AI initiative]"
2. Event hook: "Will you or your team be at [Event]?"
3. Ameya offer: "I'll be hosting Executive Briefings with our Field CTO, Ameya Bhatawdekar"
4. Topic bullets (3 max, matched to their role):
   - Trends we're seeing across the industry
   - Common failure modes with [agents/evals/observability] and how teams fix them
   - [Role-specific topic]
5. Logos matched to industry
6. CTA: "Are you open to meeting us there?"

**Approved example (VIP Blitz / Korn Ferry — Bryan launched same day):**
```
Hi Bryan,

I'm Bryan and lead the GTM team at Braintrust and have been following Korn Ferry's AI strategy and transformation work. Will you or your team be at HumanX?

I'll be hosting Executive Briefings with our Field CTO, Ameya Bhatawdekar.

Topics I thought you might be interested in:
- Trends we are seeing across the industry
- Common failure modes with agents and how teams are improving them
- Aligning product, engineering, and business teams around measurable AI quality

AI teams at OpenAI, Netflix, Lovable, Microsoft, Instacart, Robinhood, Ramp, Stripe, Airtable, Notion, and more use Braintrust to ship higher quality AI features faster.

Are you open to meeting us there?
```

**Note:** The personalization in line 1 MUST be specific ("Korn Ferry's AI strategy and transformation work" not "Korn Ferry's AI initiatives"). Do the research to make this specific.

### Pattern 3: Re-engagement (Stalled Deal)

**Format:** Short, 3-4 sentences, NO "let's go to POC" language
**When:** Deal is stalled, economic buyer has gone quiet, AE needs exec-to-exec touch
**Jay's guidance:** "Less about 'lets go to POC' and more around prioritization of this project"

**Structure:**
1. Brief context acknowledgment (relationship exists, don't oversell it)
2. Reference a company signal or Braintrust update that's relevant (Series B, new product, industry trend)
3. Prioritization question — not "are you ready to buy" but "when does it make sense to revisit this"
4. Optional: event hook if one exists nearby

**Template:**
```
Hi [Name],

[First name from AE] mentioned you've been evaluating [use case]. Wanted to reach out directly.

We just [Series B / new product launch / relevant company update]. Seeing a lot of teams at [matched logos] using this moment to get their eval infrastructure in place before scaling further.

Curious when it makes sense to put this back on the priority list. Happy to get [AE name] and the team together for a quick alignment call.

Bryan
```

## What Bryan Does NOT Want

- Generic opener: "I've been following your company's AI journey" (use the actual initiative)
- Same logo list for everyone (match to industry)
- Bullets in a cold DM (only in event briefing format)
- Vague pain: "help with your AI strategy" (name the actual pain: agent drift, eval gaps, deployment speed)
- Hard pitch: "schedule a demo" or "start a trial" (say "swap notes" or "20 minutes")
- Over-long: more than 10 sentences is too much
- No proof points: a note with no logos or customer stats will get pushed back
