# Event Invite Template

## Event Types

1. **Conference (HumanX, AI Engineer)** — open, can still attend
2. **Afterparty (Aquarium, Happy Hours)** — capacity-controlled
3. **VIP Dinner (Seattle AI Leaders, Engineering Leaders)** — invite-only, VP+ only
4. **Builders Night (Seattle, SF)** — open, broader audience
5. **Premium (F1 Miami, World Cup, Wimbledon, Warriors)** — economic buyers and champions ONLY

## Rules

- Check contact geography before inviting to local events
- Premium events (F1, World Cup, Warriors) = economic buyers and champions ONLY. Not ICs.
- VIP dinners = Director+ minimum
- Builders nights = ICs welcome
- Always include the registration link with https:// prefix
- Reference who else is attending (social proof)

## Structure

```
hey [name], [1 sentence about the event with date/location].

[1 sentence social proof: who else is there or what will be discussed].

[1 sentence personal relevance: why this matters for their specific work].

[registration link]

Kensington
```

## Example (Seattle AI Leaders Dinner)

```
hey [name], we're hosting an intimate AI leaders dinner in seattle on april 22 for VPs and engineering heads. the room includes folks from Apple, Google, DoorDash, Ford, and GE Healthcare. it's invite-only and nearly full so I can't guarantee a seat, but I'd love to share what those teams are thinking about around evals and AI reliability. open to a quick call next week?
```

## Self-Review
