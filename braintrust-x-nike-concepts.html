Design Philosophy
Built on Intelligence

Node Grid Pattern

The Braintrust icon's four-square grid is the DNA of every design — appearing in outsole traction, panel stitching, and heel branding across all four silhouettes.

Gradient Language

Every colorway transitions from Braintrust Blue (#2C1FEA) through the spectrum — representing the shift from ambiguity to clarity, from vibes to evals.

Execution Layer Sole

The midsole unit across all models features a visible grid structure — the "execution layer" that sits between intention and ground contact. Performance you can see.

Zero Waste Ethos

Inspired by the "Zero Person" concept — maximum output, minimum waste. Sustainable materials, minimal colorways, and packaging made from recycled eval printouts.
