## Archive Strategy

### When to Move a Draft to Complete:
- Email sent to intended recipient(s)
- Post published to LinkedIn/X
- Research summary delivered to team

### When to Move a Draft to Good Examples:
- Email outperforms baseline (reply rate >15% for cold, >40% for warm)
- Post gets >5% engagement rate on LinkedIn or >2% on X
- Research summary was useful and might be repurposed

### When to Delete:
- Email underperforms baseline for 3+ weeks
- Post gets <0.5% engagement after 3 days
- Research no longer relevant
- Approach or angle proved ineffective

## Performance Baselines (Update Quarterly)

**Cold Email:**
- Open rate: 20-30%
- Reply rate: 5-10%
- Meeting booked: 0.5-1%

**LinkedIn Message (warm outreach):**
- Response rate: 15-25%
- Meeting booked: 2-5%

**LinkedIn Post:**
- Engagement rate: 3-5%
- Profile visits: 10-20 from single post

**X Thread:**
- Engagement rate: 1-2%
- Impressions: 500-1,000 per post

## Seasonal Considerations

**Best Windows for Outreach:**
- **Q1 (Jan-Mar):** New year budgets, hiring plans, planning cycles
- **Q2 (Apr-Jun):** Mid-year check-ins, funding announcements
- **Q3 (Jul-Sep):** H2 planning, back-to-school hiring, conference season
- **Q4 (Oct-Dec):** Year-end reviews, bonus/planning cycles (but lower response rates)

**Avoid:**
- Friday afternoons (low engagement, delete/archive)
- Week of holidays (Thanksgiving, Christmas, July 4th, etc.)
- First week of month (budgeting meetings)
