# Writing Index

**Purpose:** Master index and filing system for all writing assets—emails, posts, messages, snippets, and experiments. This is your source of truth for where everything lives and how to organize new work.

**How to Use:** Reference this index when starting a new writing project. Use the filing rules to determine where each asset belongs. Regularly audit drafts and promote strong performers to the Published or Good Examples archive.

## Filing Rules

### Drafts
- **Location:** Referenced in DRAFTS_INDEX.md (not stored as separate files)
- **Status:** Active, unfinished, or in feedback cycle
- **Examples:** Email sequences in progress, multi-part post series, research summaries under review
- **Rotation:** Move to Published or archive after sending/posting. Update DRAFTS_INDEX monthly.

### Published
- **Definition:** Sent emails, posted content, or messages that went live
- **Archive method:** Snapshot titles, timestamps, and performance notes in GOOD_EXAMPLES.md if they perform well
- **Retention:** Keep recent (last 3 months) in active memory; older items can be archived externally

### Snippets
- **Location:** `snippets/` folder (OPENERS.md, CTAS.md, SNIPPETS.md)
- **Definition:** Reusable blocks of copy, openers, CTAs, and phrases
- **Usage:** Copy-paste and customize for each outreach. Don't send as-is; always personalize.
- **Maintenance:** Add successful patterns to snippets quarterly. Remove underperformers.

### Experiments
- **Definition:** New angles, tones, or approaches being tested
- **Status:** Tag with "TEST-[DATE]" in draft title
- **Metrics:** Track open rates, response rates, or engagement for 10+ sends before assessing
- **Promotion:** If experiment outperforms baseline by 20%+, move successful language to Snippets

## Content Organization by Type

### Cold Email
- Openers: OPENERS.md (Signal, Insight, Question, Direct)
- CTAs: CTAS.md (Soft, Medium, Hard)
- Full sequences: DRAFTS_INDEX.md (active) or GOOD_EXAMPLES.md (proven)

### Social (LinkedIn/X)
- Ideas & hooks: POST_IDEAS.md
- Reusable phrases: SNIPPETS.md
- High-performing posts: GOOD_EXAMPLES.md

### Internal/Slack
- Tone guide: TONE_GUIDE.md
- No snippets needed (context-specific)

## Key Metrics to Track

- **Email:** Open rate, reply rate, meeting booked rate
- **LinkedIn:** Profile views, engagement (likes/comments), DM opens
- **X:** Impressions, engagement rate, profile clicks
- **Overall:** Response quality (vs. spam replies)
